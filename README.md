# Utility Application Made For But Not Limited To Personal Trainer Stefan Stojiljković

Developed an Android application for a personal trainer to help clients track and manage their workouts. The app includes a customizable workout input screen, a workout history view organized by date, basic settings and contact pages. Designed for ease of use and adaptability, it is actively used by all of the trainer’s clients and is publicly available on the Google Play Store.

Google Play Store Link: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=rs.nikolaivanovic.ssfitnessdnevniktreninga&hl=en
